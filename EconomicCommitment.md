# Economic Commitment

**rdfs:label:** Economic Commitment  
**rdfs:subClassOf:** Commitment  
**rdf:type:** owl:Class

## Definition
A Commitment specifically about the transfer or transformation of Economic Resources, anticipating a future Economic Event.

## Notes
Often used in trading-partner models (e.g., sales order, purchase order lines) or internal production planning.
