# Exchange

**rdfs:label:** Exchange  
**rdf:type:** owl:Class

## Definition
A business process pattern in which agents give up one Economic Resource while receiving another, expressed via paired decrement and increment Economic Events (and often reciprocal Commitments).

## Related
- `duality` (between events)
- `reciprocity` (between commitments)
