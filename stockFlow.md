# stockFlow

**rdfs:label:** stockFlow  
**rdf:type:** owl:ObjectProperty

## Definition
Links a continuant (Economic Resource) to an occurrent (Economic Event) in a relationship that maintains data consistency (via triggered or adjusting procedures). In the trading-partner view, decomposes into inflow/outflow; outflow into give/consume/use; inflow into take/produce.

## Domain / Range
- **Domain:** Economic Event  
- **Range:** Economic Resource

## Roles
- May be specialized as: `give`, `take` in exchanges
- `consume`/`use`, `produce` in transformations
- `reservation`/`expectation` for commitments
