# Contract

**rdfs:label:** Contract  
**rdf:type:** owl:Class

## Definition
A bundle of Commitments and terms negotiated between participating Agents that governs future Economic Events.

## Key Characteristics
- Aggregates reciprocal Commitments
- Establishes terms such as pricing, timing, penalties, rights/obligations

## Related
- `governs` (Contract → Commitment)
- `partyTo` (Agent ↔ Contract)
