Definition

An association that reserves or earmarks an Economic Resource for a future Economic Event promised by a Commitment.
Domain / Range

    Domain: Economic Commitment
    Range: Economic Resource

Notes

Used to anticipate availability and manage allocations before events occur.
A subtype of Outflow which is a subtype of Stockflow
