# duality

**rdfs:label:** duality  
**rdf:type:** owl:ObjectProperty (recursive)

## Definition
Links requited Economic Events where one flow is the economic or legal consideration given/obtained for the other. One event plays the role of **increment**, the other **decrement**.

## Domain / Range
- **Domain:** Economic Event  
- **Range:** Economic Event

## Notes
- Recursive association on the Economic Event class
- Captures the why between paired events in a business process
