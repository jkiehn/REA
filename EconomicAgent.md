# Economic Agent

**rdfs:label:** Economic Agent  
**rdf:type:** owl:Class

## Definition
Identifiable parties (1) with discretionary power to use or dispose of Economic Resources, or (2) who are responsible for subordinates’ use or disposition.

## Intended Meaning
Covers both human and organizational actors that participate in Economic Events and assume responsibility relationships.

## Subtypes
- **Person** — human agents who can participate in exchanges/conversions and make commitments.
- **Economic Unit** — non-human agents such as companies, divisions, departments.

## Related
- `insideParticipate` / `outsideParticipate` (to Economic Event)
- `responsible` (Agent ↔ Agent; roles: superior/subordinate)
- `assign` (Person ↔ Economic Unit)
