# Economic Resource

**rdfs:label:** Economic Resource  
**rdf:type:** owl:Class

## Definition
Groups of objects that (1) are scarce and have utility and (2) are under the control of an Economic Agent. May be subtyped into goods, services, rights, or combinations thereof.

## Intended Meaning
Represents continuants owned/controlled by agents that can increase or decrease via Economic Events.

## Key Characteristics
- Scarcity and utility
- Under control of an Economic Agent
- Can be linked to Economic Events via `stockFlow`

## Related
- `stockFlow` (association to Economic Event)
- Subtypes: Goods, Services, Rights (optional)

## Notes
Continuant vs. occurrent distinction applies: resources are continuants; events are occurrents.
